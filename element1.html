The Coconut Consulting Academy (TCCA) is my first startup built on the mission of helping worldwide students to apply to American universities. With a dedicated three-member board of committee, we recruit and lead employees from Ivy Leagues supervise students with their application process. Our model is simple yet effective: Each student will be assigned a group of “advisors” who guided them through every step of the United States race: the essays, recommendation letters, school lists, etc. Throughout the process, we ask for feedback from our clients throughout to ensure the best and most satisfying. Coconut Consulting Academy has been working and operating since May 2021, received more than 5000$ in revenue, and had 1000+ reaches in social media platforms. Still, the start-up is constantly developing and adapting to fulfil the needs of high school students to study abroad in the United States.

Other interest: Cuisine

$300 could get me a PlayStation4, a pair of Nike Air Jordans, or the latest iPod. Despite the allure of consumerism, I ended up trading $300 for a red cart, a second-hand bike, rosemary, ketchup, spices, and a small grill. They have been my companion ever since.

That was far from my first trade-off. To my parents’ bewilderment, I never once hesitated to sacrifice Lego blocks, Tamagochi, or Spider-Man action figures for a cast iron pan. Despite my immense love for food, I was a terrible cook. My first steak, as my mom remarked, was burnt on the outside, under-seasoned, and tasted like rubber, and my second try was not much better - still tasteless and undercooked. At that moment, mom would always say that it was nonsensical for me to cook and discouraged me every time I put my heart on the plate. Finally, after trying hundreds of times, I eventually achieved what I perceived as a sensational steak. All the techniques, from searing outside the steak to the seasoning, everything was totally “perfect.”

My Favorite dish

Below is the dish I especially like cooking for family rituals or anniversaries.
